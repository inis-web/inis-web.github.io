Invitation to Support ICSME 2016

ICSME is the premier international venue in software maintenance and evolution, where researchers and practitioners from academia, industry, and government have met annually for the past 31 years. The 2016 conference will be held in downtown Raleigh, North Carolina, USA.

Corporate contributions are key to the success of conferences such as ICSME 2016. We invite you to support the conference as a corporate benefactor. Supporting ICSME is a great way to obtain or enhance your visibility among a large group of leading scientists and outstanding software engineers.

Key facts and figures about ICSME 2014 in Victoria, British Columbia, Canada include:

  • 308 attendees from over 30 countries
  • >25% of attendees were students
  • 180+ papers by 425+ authors were presented at ICSME and its co-located events
  • 200+ volunteers contributed to the organization of ICSME and its co-located events
